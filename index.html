The Cat Ark

Hello!

The Cat Ark is a voluntary cat rescue in Galway, Ireland. We began looking after and rehoming local cats without homes in 2018. Since then we have helped many cats to find their happy-ever-afters. We also humanely trap wild cats so our vet can vaccinate and neuter or spay them. When they have recovered they are then returned to their area. Cats and kittens that are more socialised are taken in to be rehomed. Neutering cats is good for their health and prevents overpopulation. Mating behaviors cease, like roaming, yowling, spraying, and fighting.
