Hello!

The Cat Ark is a voluntary cat rescue in Galway, Ireland. We began looking after and rehoming local cats without homes in 2018. Since then we have helped many cats to find their happy-ever-afters.

We also humanely trap wild cats so our vet can vaccinate and neuter or spay them. When they have recovered they are then returned to their area. Cats and kittens that are more socialised are taken in to be rehomed. Neutering cats is good for their health and prevents overpopulation. Mating behaviors cease, like roaming, yowling, spraying, and fighting.

Adopt a Furry Friend!

Adopting a cat from The Cat Ark will give them a chance at a loving home, while also making it possible for us to help another cat by freeing up a space at our resce shelter. We will do our best to help match you with the right cat for you and we will be here to support you whenever you need us. All of our cats have been vet checked, vaccinated and neutered or spayed, ensuring your new companion is in its best health. We also microchip all of our cats which gives you a great chance of being reunited should your cat ever wander.
